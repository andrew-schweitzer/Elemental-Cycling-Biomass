METEOR:

    F(t) = F0 + (F1 - F0)^(-t/tao)
    tao: 150 Ma
    above formula is utilized for cometary impact and impact fixation

    Nitrogen:
        cometary_input(t) = CI0 + (CI1 - CI0)^(-t/tao)
            CI0 = 2.4e5
            CI1 = 2.4e8
            tao = 150 Ma
        cometary_fixation(t) = CF0 + (CF1 - CF0)^(-t/tao)
            CF0 = 4.7e6
            CF1 = 4.8e9
            tao = 150 Ma

Ocean-Atmosphere-Equilibrium:

    K = p / c
        p: partial pressure of specie in Atmosphere
        c: concentration in Ocean
        assume constant average ocean temp to be 25C
        K_N2 = 1600 L*atm/mol

Marine-sediment-absorption:

    Q = Kf*( (Ce)^(1/n) )
    Q: amt of solute absorbed per mass of sediment
    Kf: absorption capacity (generally Kf < 1)
    n: Freundlich coefficient (generally n < 1)
        n ~ 1
        Kf ~ 1e-4

    Crust_Continential(t) = Crust_Continential.N(t-1) + Q
    Ocean(t) = Ocean(t-1) - Q
    
Continential-Erosion:

    F_sed_mantle = (1-epsilon)*(M_sed / tao)
    epsilon: fraction recycled to cont_crust instead of mantle
        range(0,1,.01)
    tao: subduction rate 
        range(1e-5, 5e-5)
    M_sed: mass of nitrogen in sediments

Erosion:

    Crust_Continential(t) = Crust_Continential(t-1)*(1 - Erosion_rate)
    Ocean(t) = Crust_Continential(t-1)*(Erosion_rate)
